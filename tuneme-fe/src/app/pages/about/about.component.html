About Tune Me

Tune Me is a mobile-first platform that provides young people with relevant, accurate, and engaging information about sexual and reproductive health and rights. It aims to empower young people to make informed decisions about their bodies and lives.

Features

Tune Me offers a variety of features to help you learn and grow:

  • Interactive articles and quizzes on sexual health topics.
  • A Q&A section where you can ask experts your questions anonymously.
  • Personal stories and experiences from other young people.
  • Information on where to find sexual health services near you.
  • Competitions and challenges to test your knowledge and win prizes.

Users

Join the Tune Me community by clicking on the profile icon to log in and create a username. You'll be able to comment on articles, ask experts your sexual health questions and enter our Story writing competitions.

Partners

Tune Me was built and is operated by Praekelt Foundation. Funding for this site was provided by United Nations Population Fund (UNFPA) and the Ford Foundation.

UNFPA advocates for the rights of young people, including the right to relevant and accurate information about sexual and reproductive health.

The Ford Foundation supports sexual and reproductive health policies, innovative programmes and research that address social, cultural and economic factors that hinder improved outcomes for women and girls.
